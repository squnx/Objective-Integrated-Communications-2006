Interactive Project Manager
We are a design agency with an expanding interactive department. We are looking to hire a talented, seasoned leader to join our team. You will be responsible for working with multiple staff, partners, clients and agency stakeholders. The ideal candidate will have agency experience on international Fortune 500 accounts. S/he will have advanced skills in project scoping, project management, and strategic planning. Cross functional teams that the candidate will be working with may include IT, programming, marketing, design, editorial and the publishing teams. S/he will set project scope, work with team to meet timelines, identify resources needed, assign tasks/responsibilities and keep an updated (weekly) forecast of the work flow and project progress for all involved parties.

Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Manage web development process for multiple clients
  • Contribute to the process of planning and strategic development
  • Plan, set, and meet deadlines
  • Manage design/development/production staff to ensure that multiple projects remain on schedule and within budget
  • Accurately forecast production/development requirements based on business requirements
  • Develop and manage relationships with clients, contractors and in-house resources
  • Conduct regular weekly status calls with multiple clients in U.S., Canada, and E.U.
  • Develop and maintain internal production schedules on a weekly basis
  • Manage multiple projects and production staff to ensure maximum productivity against deadlines
  • Track progress of production staff member assigned project tasks
  • Move projects through from requirements gathering to design to implementation, testing and delivery
  • Work with Account Executives to ensure project deliverables are clearly defined, responsibilities are established for development, and quality assurance and the final product achieves the business requirements
  • Define and track, in writing, the tasks, dependencies, priorities, milestones, deliverables and risks associated with each project
  • Conduct regular (daily and weekly) status meetings with agency principles as well as other departments to coordinate project release cycles
  • Create user-flow diagrams, site maps, and detailed wireframes based on project requirement and client requests
  • Edit HTML/CSS code as needed
  • Provide original insight and strategic thinking to agency approach and process
  • Use online project management tools to manage process and communication
  • Communicate directly with offshore developers
  • Conduct research on industry topics, technology, best practices, and developments as assigned, and communicate findings and recommendations to the larger team

Qualifications:
  • BA/BS or equivalent combination of relevant education and work experience required
  • Excellent communication, organizational and interpersonal skills
  • Intermediate to advanced computer skills; intermediate to advanced knowledge of .Net development
  • Excellent project management skills
  • Demonstrated ability to own projects and work independently to drive projects and initiatives through to completion
  • Proven track record of managing multiple projects simultaneously
  • Comfortable with ambiguity and taking on projects that require exploring uncharted territory
  • Ability to work cross functionally with a varied set of internal stakeholders
  • Strong organizational, teamwork and time management skills
  • Demonstrated interest in the interactive industry, and eagerness to learn new technical knowledge and skills
  • Ability to manage/assign work to outside vendors
  • Availability to occasionally work odd hours to communicate directly with offshore development teams via IM and email on critical issues
  • Extremely detail oriented and organized
  • Ability to conduct and direct intensive and detailed QA, including bug reporting, with clear annotated descriptions for offshore developers
  • 5+ years of experience with interactive media development
  • Experience with planning and implementing large scale e-commerce projects
  • Experience with project management tools like Basecamp, MS Project, etc...
  • Knowledge of web analytics and reporting, and implementation of tracking codes
  • Hands-on HTML/CSS programming skills is strongly preferred
  • Intermediate to advanced knowledge of mobile technologies/platforms, development, and processes
  • Intermediate to advanced knowledge of social media and social integration, optimization, implementation, and best practices
  • Availability to travel 2-3 times a year to participate in client meetings or attend industry conferences

Requirements for consideration:
  • Examples of user-flow diagrams, site maps and/or wireframes
  • Examples of 4 URLs and/or mobile/social apps with summaries of responsibilities on each project
  • Examples of written project scopes, technical documentation, or other related content (e.g., blog, whitepaper, etc...)
  • Completion of the attached questionnaire
  • Three references with contact information (phone and email)

Senior Art Director: Web/Print
We are looking for a Senior Art Director--an experienced creative who isn't afraid to dig into both web and print projects with both hands.

The Senior Art Director is one of the most pivotal positions in the agency. The individual is responsible for turning complex creative briefs into award winning creative. They must be able to work with a multi-disciplinary team to not only achieve project goals but to exceed client and agency expectations.

Assignments range from interactive campaigns to brand identity to packaging design. Art directors who come out of design backgrounds are best suited for this position. Many agency projects include online and off line components, so print and web production should be second nature.

Responsibilities include:

  • Concept and campaign development
  • Managing project resources such as in-house team members, freelancers, and production artists
  • Client presentation development
  • Ensuring client brand standards in all work
  • Working with the development team
  • Turning wireframes into site designs
  • Upholding agency design standards
  • Brand system development
  • Tracking and approving completed art assets
  • Working with interactive team to adjust project goals and design revisions

Considerations:

  • We are looking for someone in the Los Angeles or Pasadena area. No out of state applicants please.

Salary is $80k

Requirements:
  • Expert knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite
  • Working knowledge of web production tools and processes.
